Library Services

Research Resources

Access our extensive collection of databases, e-journals, and digital archives to support your research needs. Our team is available to assist you in finding and utilizing these resources effectively.

Research Guides

Explore our curated research guides tailored to specific subjects and disciplines. These guides provide valuable resources, search strategies, and expert tips to enhance your research process.

Circulation & Borrowing

Learn about our borrowing policies, loan periods, and how to check out materials. We offer a range of services to ensure you have access to the resources you need for your research.

Copying/Printing/Scanning

Utilize our self-service copying, printing, and scanning facilities. We provide high-quality equipment to support your document needs, with options for color and large-format printing.

Information Literacy

Enhance your research skills with our information literacy programs. We offer workshops, tutorials, and one-on-one sessions to help you navigate the complex world of information resources.

Interlibrary Loan

Access materials from other libraries worldwide through our interlibrary loan service. If we don't have what you need, we'll do our best to obtain it from another institution.

Reserves

Find course materials placed on reserve by your instructors. Our reserve system ensures equitable access to high-demand resources for all students.

Spaces

Discover our variety of study and collaboration spaces. From quiet individual study carrels to group project rooms equipped with technology, we have spaces to suit all your research needs.
