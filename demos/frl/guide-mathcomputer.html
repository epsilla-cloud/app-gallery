Mathematics and Computer Science Research Guide

General Information

This is a guide to selected sources of information about Mathematics and Computer Science at FRL.

  • Subject-specific materials and resources, such as books, articles, and research tools.
  • Citation style guides for your subject.

Research Papers

  • ACM Digital Library

    Full-text articles from journals in computing and information technology.

  • MathSciNet

    Math and physics literature 1940-.

  • ProQuest Computing Database

    Journal articles on database design, software development, web commerce, LANs, WANs, intranets and the internet. Included in ProQuest Central.

  • IEEE Xplore (IEEE)

    Full-text access to IEEE journals, magazines, transactions, proceedings from approximately 125 of the most important IEEE conferences, and selected e-books.

  • ProQuest Dissertations & Theses Citation Index (Clarivate)

    Cited references for dissertations included in the ProQuest Dissertations and Theses database. Useful for locating unpublished and new research.

  • ScienceDirect (Elsevier)

    A collection of journals from the publisher Elsevier and its affiliates.

  • Web of Science (Science Citation Index)

    Leading scholarly literature in the science disciplines.

Reference Works

  • Credo Reference

    Reference sources for all subjects, including encyclopedias, dictionaries, thesauri, quotations, etc.

  • Free Programming Books (Ebook Foundation)

    Links to CC-BY-licensed books about programming, and additional online resources.

  • Free Science Books (Ebook Foundation)

    Links to CC-BY-licensed books on scientific topics including mathematics.

Review Articles

  • Annual Reviews

    Literature reviews for many different disciplines including physical sciences, and social sciences.

Media Collections

  • Films on Demand

    Streaming video collection of educational programs in the humanities, social sciences, and sciences. Content is provided by the BBC, the Canadian Broadcast Corp, Films for the Humanities & Sciences, PBS, and others.

Online Learning

  • O'Reilly Online Learning

    Technology and business ebooks, case studies, videos, and learning paths.
